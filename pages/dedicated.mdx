# Uffizzi Dedicated

Uffizzi Dedicated is our single-tenant, SaaS offering designed for organizations that require dedicated infrastucture and a private instance of Uffizzi managed by our team. Uffizzi Dedicated is ideal for organizations that require a high level of privacy, customization, and control over their Uffizzi environments.

Dedicated customer workloads run within a private project and on dedicated infrastructure that can be customized for their every use case and in accordance with their specific cloud resource requirements. This enables Uffizzi Dedicated to stand up their developer platform in minutes, to take advantages of Uffizzi's ongoing innovation, and to reduce staffing requirement burdens of building and maintaining your own developer platform.
